emergent
word📖
/ɪˈmɜːrdʒənt/
adjective
Definition (EN)
Newly arising or coming into prominence; (in AI/systems theory) describing properties that arise from a complex system but cannot be predicted from its individual components.
释义 (ZH)
新兴的；涌现的 — 在复杂系统中自发出现、无法从单个组件预测的属性或行为。常见于人工智能语境，描述随模型规模扩大而突然出现的能力。
