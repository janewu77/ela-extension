Expert Translation

Not just translated.
Actually understood.

Most translators give you words. ELA gives you understanding. A built-in 3-step methodology — literal, review, rewrite — produces translations that are accurate, fluent, and fully explained.

  • Step 1: Literal translation — preserves structure and meaning
  • Step 2: AI review — flags awkward phrasing and translation issues
  • Step 3: Idiomatic rewrite — polished, native-speaker quality
  • Fully customizable for any language pair

How it works

Up and running
in three steps

ELA fits naturally into your reading flow. No tab-switching, no copy-pasting. Just select and learn.

1

Select any text

Highlight a word, sentence, or whole paragraph on any webpage — an article, a forum post, a document, anything.

Works on any website
2

Open the side panel

ELA lives in Chrome's side panel. Press ⌘ Shift S (or click the icon) to open it alongside your page — no interruption to your reading.

⌘ Shift S
3

Click and learn

Choose TTS to hear it, Word Lookup to dissect it, Translation to understand it, or a custom action to process it your way.
